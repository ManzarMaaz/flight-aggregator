GLOBAL SKY AIRLINES - OFFICIAL PASSENGER POLICY 2026

1. PET POLICY (CABIN AND CARGO)
Passengers are allowed to bring small pets (dogs and cats only) into the aircraft cabin. The pet must remain inside a TSA-approved carrier that fits under the seat in front of you. A non-refundable pet fee of $125 applies per flight segment. Iguanas, snakes, and large birds are strictly prohibited from all flights.

2. CANCELLATION AND REFUNDS
If you cancel your flight within 24 hours of the initial booking, you are entitled to a full refund to your original payment method. If you cancel after the 24-hour grace period, a $50 cancellation fee will be deducted, and the remaining balance will be issued as a travel credit valid for one year.
